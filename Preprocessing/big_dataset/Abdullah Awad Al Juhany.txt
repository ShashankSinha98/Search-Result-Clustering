Sheikh Abdullah Awad Al Juhany (Arabic: عبد الله عواد الجهني), is one of the Imams of the Grand Mosque Masjid al-Haram in Mecca. He holds a B.A. from the faculty of Qur'an at The Islamic University of Madinah, and a doctorate (Ph.D.) from Umm al-Qura University in Mecca.Juhany has led the Taraweeh prayers during Ramadan in Mecca since 2005, and is known for his emotional Quranic recitation style. His voice has been widely recorded and is internationally distributed by various communities.

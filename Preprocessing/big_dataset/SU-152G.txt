The SU-152G (Russian: СУ-152Г, GABTU index "Object 108") is a Soviet experimental 152-mm self-propelled howitzer, and is designed by OKB-3 of the heavy machine construction division of Uralmash. The main designer of the SU-152G is Lev Gorlitsky. The SU-152G was intended to suppress and destroy enemy firing positions, engage armored hostiles as well as area denial, in addition to conducting counter-battery tasks.

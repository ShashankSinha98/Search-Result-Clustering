Abdullah Azwar Anas (born 6 August 1973) is an Indonesian politician who served as the regent Banyuwangi, East Java, between 2010-2015 and 2016-2021. Prior to becoming a regent, he had served as both an elected member of the People's Representative Council and an appointment to the People's Consultative Assembly, for one term each.
Born in Banyuwangi, Anas completed his education at the University of Indonesia before joining the Consultative Assembly for 2 years. Later, he was elected in 2004 for a five-year term in the legislative council, and was elected as regent of Banyuwangi after failing to win his re-election.

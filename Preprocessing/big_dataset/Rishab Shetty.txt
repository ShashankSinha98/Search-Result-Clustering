Prashant Shetty known professionally as Rishab Shetty is an Indian director and actor, born on July 7 who primarily works in Kannada-language cinema. He is a diploma holder in film direction from the Government Film and TV Institute in Bangalore and has worked with A. M. R. Ramesh on Cyanide.Shetty then assisted Arvind Kaushik on a TV series and went on to make his acting debut in Thuglak. He played a small role as a police officer in Pawan Kumar's Lucia. Rishab then played a key role in Ulidavaru Kandante, a movie directed by Rakshit Shetty.
Shetty has a background in yakshagana, a theatrical folk art from coastal Karnataka.

The first film he produced was A Man Called Tiger from 1973. In 1989, he was nominated for a Hong Kong Film Award for best picture for the movie Painted Faces, which was released in 1988. Later productions credits include Armour of God and Rumble in the Bronx.
In the end credits of the 2012 movie CZ12, Jackie Chan cited Leonard Ho as his mentor.
Ho was awarded Hong Kong Film Award for Lifetime Achievement in 1999.

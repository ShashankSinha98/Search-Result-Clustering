Modrzewo (Polish: [mɔdˈʐɛvɔ]; German: Moderow) is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Suchań, within Stargard County, West Pomeranian Voivodeship, in north-western Poland. It lies approximately 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east of Suchań, 24 km (15 mi) east of Stargard, and 56 km (35 mi) east of the regional capital Szczecin.
For the history of the region, see History of Pomerania.

Herman Haar House, also known as the Haar-Bergman House and Byrd House, is a historic home located at Jefferson City, Cole County, Missouri. It was built about 1859, and is a 1+1⁄2-story, four bay, Missouri-German Vernacular brick dwelling.  It has a gable roof and two front doors. It was moved in 1986 to its present location.: 5 It was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1997.

Michelle Goh (Chinese: 吴怡慧; pinyin: Wú Yí Huì, born 9 September 1973), is a Singaporean actress and entrepreneur. She left MediaCorp in 1998 for Vancouver and has been staying in Canada for 10 years. She founded Float House in 2014, a floating therapy wellness centre at Novena Medical Centre, Float House ceased business on 2016.She has appeared in Da Vinci's Inquest (1998 - 2005), Smallville (2001), Sacred, Out for a Kill (2003), Alien Lockdown (2004) and Human Cargo (2004), and  as well as other films and TV episodes.

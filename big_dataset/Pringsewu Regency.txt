Pringsewu Regency is a regency of Lampung Province, Sumatra, Indonesia. It has an area of 625 kmÂ² and had a population of 364,825 people at the 2010 Census and 405,466 at the 2020 Census. The regency seat is the town of Pringsewu, located 37 kilometres from the provincial capital of Bandar Lampung. The regency was created on 29 October 2008.The regency name came from Javanese language terms "Thousand (sewu) bamboo (pring)", as the location of the town was once a bamboo forest.

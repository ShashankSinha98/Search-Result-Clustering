Oricum (Ancient Greek: Ὤρικον, Ὤρικος or Ὠρικός; Latin: Oricum or Oricus; Albanian: Oriku or Orikum) was an Ancient Greek city at the south end of the Bay of Vlorë on the southern Adriatic coast between Epirus and Illyria. It is located near modern day Orikum, Vlorë County, Albania. The site of Oricum is an archaeological park of Albania.

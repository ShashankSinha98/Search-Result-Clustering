Michelle Ann Jane Goszko (born 7 October 1977 in Blacktown, New South Wales) is a former Australian cricketer.  A right-handed batter and occasional right-arm medium pace bowler, she played 4 Test matches for Australia between 2001 and 2006, scoring 217 runs, a low return after making a double-century on her Test debut against England in June 2001.  She has also played 34 One Day Internationals for Australia, scoring 669 runs with an average in the mid-twenties.

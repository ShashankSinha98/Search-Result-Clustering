Vitaliy Stanislavovych Koval (Ukrainian: Віталій Станіславович Коваль; born 28 July 1981  in Berezne, Rivne oblast) is a Ukrainian entrepreneur and politician.  Vice-president of the Ukrainian Wrestling Association. Member of the National Olympic Committee of Ukraine.
Head of the Rivne Regional State Administration since September 9, 2019 Governor of Rivne Oblast.

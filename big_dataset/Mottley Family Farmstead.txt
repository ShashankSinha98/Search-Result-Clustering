The Mottley Family Farmstead on Ivey Road in Willow Springs in Lafayette County, Wisconsin is a 8 acres (3.2 ha) property which was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 2001.  The listing included three contributing buildings.It is an abandoned farm on an isolated ravine, begun in 1854 by English immigrants Abraham and Susan Motley.

Jaber Ebne Hayyan Pharmaceutical Company (Persian: شرکت داروسازی جابر ابن حیان) engages in manufacture and sale of injection and oral antibiotics, ointments, inhalers, nasal sprays & pearls (soft gel capsules). The company was formerly known as Squibb Iran and changed its name in 1979. Jaber Ebne Hayyan Pharmaceutical Company was founded in 1960 and is based in Tehran, Iran. The company is public joint stock and member of Tehran Stock Exchange with the registered capital of 378,000,000,000 rials.

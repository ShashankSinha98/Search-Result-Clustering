Joss Jaffe (born October 1, 1980) is a musician and producer. His album Meditation Music debuted at number 3 on the Billboard New Age Albums Chart for the week of October 12, 2019. He is known for work in New Age, World music, Reggae and Electronic genres. He has collaborated with Grammy Award-winning Reggae singer Mykal Rose (AKA Michael Rose) from Jamaica and artists Jai Uttal and Donna De Lory.

Rishabh Kashyap (Hindi: ऋषभ कश्यप; born 23 March 1996), known mononymously as Golu is an Indian actor and singer, known for working in Bhojpuri films. He made his first screen appearance as a child in the 2008 film Nirahua Rickshawala, which starred Dinesh Lal Yadav. As an adult, he starred in Arvind Chaubey's Action - drama Tridev (2017) and in the romantic drama Dulhan Hum Le Jayenge (2019), which was a commercial success.
